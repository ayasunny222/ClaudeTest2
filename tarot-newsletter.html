A Message from Celine

Welcome back, dear souls. This week the cards speak in a clear and determined voice. The energy of transformation is strong — the kind that doesn't ask permission. We stand at a threshold where releasing the old is not just wise, but necessary. Breathe in. The universe has already written the next chapter. Let's read it together.

The Sun
✦

XIX — The Sun

Major Arcana • Ruled by the Sun

The Sun rises over this week with unmistakable confidence. After a period of shadows and uncertainty, clarity floods in like morning light through open curtains. This is a card of joy, vitality, and truth — and it arrives not as a reward for struggle, but as a reminder of what was always yours.

Where you have been doubting yourself, The Sun says: stop. Your instincts are correct. The project you've been nurturing, the relationship you've been tending, the dream you've been afraid to name aloud — all of these are real, and they are ready. Step forward without apology.

Celine's Weekly Ritual
⚢

Sunday Sun Activation

Align yourself with The Sun's energy this week with a simple Sunday morning ritual:

  • Find a quiet spot where natural light touches you.
  • Hold your tarot deck to your heart and take three deep breaths.
  • Pull one card and ask: "What do I need to illuminate this week?"
  • Write your card and its message in a journal. Carry that message with you.
  • Light a yellow or gold candle to honour The Sun's warmth and clarity.
